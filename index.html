Mac & Cheese Recipe

Description

A simple and delicious recipe to make the Mac and Cheese of your dreams.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Macaroni (8 ounce)
  • Quarter cup of butter
  • Quarter cup of all-purpose flour
  • Half teaspoon of salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  1. Assemble your ingredients
  2. Boil a large pot of water and cooking the macaroni for 8 minutes
  3. Melt your butter in medium heat in your pan at the same time as step 2
  4. Add flour, salt, and pepper; stir for 5 minutes or until smooth
  5. Slowly add milk and stir until smooth for 5 minutes
  6. Add cheddar cheese until melted for 2 to 4 minutes
  7. Add macaroni and fold into cheese sauce
  8. Serve your dish!
